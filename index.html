AURORA

ISTE's Annual Tech Week

About

ISTE Manipal is back with our annual TechWeek: Aurora. This year, we present to you student workshops on several technical domains that range from software to hardware. In collaboration with the best student bodies from around MIT, we have created a platform for you to learn how to create hands-on projects in your field of interest from scratch!

Workshops: The temple of learning for those bold enough to be pushing their boundaries. Workshops will be held for many learning communities of ISTE, from AI all th way to Web Development. A hands-on project will be provided to ensure that the contents of the workshop are solidified with the candidates.

Competitions: Are you brave enough to challenge the best of Manipal and all over in technical skills? Test yourself and your technical skills against others at our TechWeek competition. Ranging across all domains imaginable, we have a wide array of prizes waiting for you. Question is, are you ready?

Hackathons: Creation and Innovation go hand in hand. Challenge your creativity at the Hackathons and test your creativity against people from all over. Work in teams (or alone) to solve a real life problem using the most basic gifts given to a person: Curiosity and Creativity.

Guest Speakers: 18th February, 2023 and 20th February, 2023
Workshops: 12th February, 2023 - 17th February, 2023
CTF Event: 17th February, 2023 - 18th February, 2023
Hackathons: 19th February, 2023
